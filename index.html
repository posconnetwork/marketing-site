Mission

To create an interactive, immersive, and realistic environment that can be used by industry professionals to innovate and test solutions to aviation's evolving challenges.

Platform

We have created a massively multiplayer online network that connects users of various flight simulators together with virtual air traffic controllers to create a highly realistic emulation of world-wide airspace.

Technology

All of our infrastructure is completely custom and includes cloud server software, VOIP technology, ATC and pilot client software, web applications, and distribution and authentication software.

Unmatched Multiplayer Experience

Membership

POSCON owns and operates a non-profit organization (POSCON, Inc.) which consists of hundreds of volunteers. As a result, we are able to provide a 100% free membership to flight simulation enthusiasts. Our goal is to simulate the world airspace system through technology. On your next flight simulation flight experience what it's like to fly anywhere in the world and immerse yourself in our highly realistic / accurate network.

Benefits of Membership

Company Exposure

Are you interested in a career as a real-world pilot or air traffic controller? Simply flying or controlling on POSCON can help you accomplish that goal by exposing your aptitude to real-world companies that are looking to hire!

Smooth Training

Ensure your real-world training is executed with precision and with minimal roadblocks. Using POSCON at home can help you come prepared to every real-world aviation training event.

Help Professionals

Make a difference in real-world aviation by helping companies test their procedures. In some cases, POSCON allows enthusiasts to participate in commercial activities. You could be making a big difference in shaping the future of aviation!

Education

There is a saying in Aviation, you never stop learning. POSCON gives you the opportunity to learn new procedures, airspace and aircraft. Our feedback system helps everyone learn from the most experienced users, to the newest.

Network Opportunities

Being able to connect and grow your relationships is key in the aviation industry. Whether your making connections to enhance your community, or wishing to grow your career, POSCON offers you the platform to do so. Many flight simulation users have networked to enhance their aviation career's.

Solutions

Pilot & ATC Recruitment

The aviation industry is in the middle of a pilot and air traffic controller shortage throughout the world. POSCON offers a venue for companies to recruit candidates that demonstrate an aptitude for working these complex jobs.

Reduce Training Costs

Initial and requalification training is a major cost in aviation. Our highly realistic software, enhanced data collection, and gamification offers the perfect training platform for pilots and air traffic controllers to practice their skills thereby minimizing time spent in the expensive Level-D sims.

Optimize Procedure Testing

By creating a realistic world airspace simulation, POSCON offers companies a comprehensive and cost effective environment solution to test new procedures in.
